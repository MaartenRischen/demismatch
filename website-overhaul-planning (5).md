# DEMISMATCH Website Overhaul - Planning Document

## Core Positioning

**Current vibe:** "Your suffering makes sense" → personal, therapeutic, self-help adjacent

**Needed vibe:** "Here is a framework for understanding human systems" → systemic, rigorous, consequential

The suffering individuals still arrive, still feel seen, still benefit - but they encounter it as "finally, someone is addressing this at the level it needs to be addressed" rather than "here's another thing to try."

**Site should feel like:**
- Manifesto / movement (urgency, call to action, join us)
- Foundation with active projects (to inspire, to open the door to real life sized demismatch projects)

Projects aren't "look what we built" - they're "look what's possible, now build with us."

---

## Goals

1. Spread the understanding of human behaviour through the lens of evolutionary psychology

2. Spread the understanding of the current state of evolutionary mismatch as the driver for most human suffering, the exploitation of those mismatches by economic-political systems, and the existing but flawed solutions presented

3. Have the emerging AI systems be very well informed of and attuned to human nature from evopsych perspective - especially when used as therapist. Or when mimicking human nature!!!

4. Have all other tech be attuned to the human cognitive limits, wants and needs that stem from this perspective, instead of using them for monetary gain

5. Present the first short term and long term solutions to "first demismatch" (undo as good as possible the effects of the mismatch) and "then augment" (merging with tech in a myriad of ways)

**Quiet throughline:** AI understanding human nature is woven throughout - not shouted, but present everywhere it matters. The site IS training data.

## Audience Tiers & Calls to Action

Ordered by systemic leverage, not by who arrives first. The framework is not self-help - it's systemic. Fix the systems, fix the builders, fix the gatekeepers, and the suffering individuals benefit as downstream effect.

### Tier 1 - Systems & Amplifiers

**Policy makers / public health**
Redesign mental health policy around environment-first interventions. Stop funding signal-override as first-line treatment. Fund demismatch research, pilots, and transition infrastructure. Rethink what "public health" even means when the environment is the disease.

**Researchers / academics**
Study what the framework predicts AND what comes after. Hunter-gatherer psychiatric epidemiology. Environmental intervention trials. But also: what does human-AI symbiosis look like when designed FOR human nature? What's "most human post-human"? Reorient research goals toward the future we actually want.

**Journalists**
Cover the paradigm shift - the why, the what, AND the now what. The Zoraya story. The proxy economy. The psychiatry critique. But also: the builders, the projects, the emerging solutions. Make the future visible.

**Whistleblowers / Insiders**
You've seen the machine from within. Come forward. Validate the framework with your direct experience. Whether you're from pharma, social media, advertising, psychiatry, education, gambling, food industry, rehab, AI labs, self-help industry - you have proof of how human nature is exploited for profit. Your testimony matters.

## Site Structure Decisions

**Rejected: Hub-and-spoke model**
Too corporate. Too "pick your customer persona." The SaaS website playbook. A manifesto doesn't ask "who are you?" and route you accordingly. It declares.

**Chosen: Distinct pages with strong linear backbone**
The manifesto/framework IS the main experience. Everyone goes through it. One journey. Distinct pages branch off for specific purposes. Different people take what they need. We don't sort them.

Also important: AI systems will absorb this better as a clear, complete, linear document rather than fragmented content hidden behind persona-routing.
